The Foodie Nest is a Northern Italian restaurant with an emphasis on quality ingredients and precision cooking. The vegetable-forward dishes pay tribute to the food of Milan with a menu that utilizes the bounty of Northern California produce and pays careful attention to detail – treating quality ingredients with respect and allowing them to shine in their best iterations. Our goal is to continue to provide a service to our community, to offer security to our staff, and to support our local food sources and suppliers.
